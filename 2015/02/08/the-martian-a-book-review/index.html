The Martian - A Book Review (Spoiler-free!)

Sunday, February 08, 2015
Posted in books, review, the martian, andy weir

Thoughts Before I Started Reading

I've become a big fan of sci-fi lately. 90% of the books I have read in the last 2 years have been related to science fiction in some way. Another topic I enjoy is space. Space is exciting, scary, unknown. Combine those things, and you get a book like The Martian. Needless to say, this book has been on my reading list since I first heard about it.

My Favorite Part

The main character's humor. This man endures so much on Mars, and yet he's still cracking jokes.

When I Got Bored

I'll substitute bored with frustrated. There are a few times when I'm yelling at the main character to hurry up. He obliges most of the time but every now and then, that causes problems.

Thoughts After I Finished Reading

What a fantastic novel. Absolutely worth my while. This book goes on my Read Me Again shelf. Can't wait for the movie.

My Recommendation

Read it before November 2015. That's when the movie is scheduled to be released and I have a feeling there will be some good parts missing from the film.
