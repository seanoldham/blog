The Times They've Been A-Changin'

Friday, May 23, 2014
Posted in spotify, python, ruby

I told you I would post a life update in a few days and here I am, keeping my word. In the past few months, I've been busy. We moved into a new place and we love it. Our commutes have been cut drastically, and the location of our place now is perfect for doing things within the city on weeknights. Whether it's taking Harvey to a hybrid bar/dogpark, going downtown for a bite and a free fireworks show, or just catching a movie at Alamo Drafthouse, everything is much more accessible.

During all this moving, I have been trying to learn some sort of programming. I started by trying to learn Python in order to automate a task at work. I got a decent bit of learning with it and can make it solve basic problems for me. Since my Python journey began, my brother and I have decided to start a business and create a website. We've tried the WordPress route and it's either too slow or too costly for what we want. Instead, I spent $12 on a theme layout and have been learning how to build a site from scratch. I know that technically a "built from scratch" site means I start with a blank page, but there is a lot more out there than I thought in terms of HTML & CSS technology and I'm clearly still in the learning phase. Having a theme laid out has made it slightly easier, but creating the functionality of the site is a job in its own.

I've shifted my efforts from Python to Ruby due to the nature of work I'm doing at the moment. Ruby seems versatile and simple enough to learn, and it appears to be able to do database tasks with relative ease. I just made the switch to Ruby so it will be at least a few weeks before any real gains can come from it. I'm having fun, though. I'm learning things that can be applied to just about any job, and may even help me in the future at my current position. There are a lot of tasks that can be automated at any workplace, and mine is no different.

So there it is, the update you've wanted. Since I rarely update my music blog, I plan to start posting here at least once a week and including a What I'm Listening To track from Spotify. So until next time, I leave you with this:
